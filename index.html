FAQ

Questions Before Launch

Clear answers for users, partners, and early testers.

Is the dashboard part of the landing page?

No. The customer dashboard belongs behind login, where users can view transfers, saved recipients, and support cases privately.

Can users send money directly from this landing page?

The landing page should explain the product and move users into WhatsApp. Actual transaction entry, PIN approval, and receipts happen in the authenticated customer flow.

What is the first MVP payment method?

Domestic mobile-money transfers through MonCash or Natcash. QR payments, photo payments, and international transfers should come after the first flow is reliable.

How does Zelnando protect users?

AI helps understand requests, but backend rules verify identity, amount, limits, recipient, AML status, and PIN approval before any transfer is created.
